Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, products, and processes

Farmers
How do I place an order for agricultural inputs?

You can place an order through our website or mobile app. Simply browse our catalog, select the products you need, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can also order via WhatsApp by sending a message to our support number.

Was this helpful?
What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments through M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, and bank transfers. For farmers, we also offer a credit system where you can get inputs now and pay after harvest.

Was this helpful?
How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 3-5 business days to most areas in Tanzania. Delivery times may vary based on your location and order size. For urgent orders, we offer express delivery options.

Was this helpful?
Do you offer training on how to use the inputs?

Yes, we provide free training sessions and resources on proper usage of agricultural inputs. You can access our educational resources section or request a training session through our support channels. We also have video tutorials available in our app.

Was this helpful?
Suppliers
How do I become a supplier for Shamba Direct?

To become a supplier, you need to register on our platform and provide necessary documentation about your business. Our team will review your application and contact you for further steps. You can start the process by visiting our supplier registration page.

Was this helpful?
What are the requirements for product quality?

All products must meet national agricultural standards and be properly certified. We require detailed product information, including ingredients, usage instructions, and safety data sheets. Products are subject to quality checks before approval for sale.

Was this helpful?
How are payments processed for suppliers?

Payments to suppliers are processed bi-weekly through electronic transfers. We require verified bank account information during registration. For any payment inquiries, you can contact our supplier support team directly.

Was this helpful?
Financial Partners
How does the credit system work for farmers?

Our credit system allows qualified farmers to purchase inputs now and pay after harvest. Farmers apply for credit based on their land size and previous farming history. Approval is typically processed within 48 hours, and repayment terms are flexible based on crop cycles.

Was this helpful?
What are the interest rates for farmer credit?

Interest rates for our credit system range from 8-12% annually, depending on the farmer's credit history and the type of inputs purchased. First-time users may qualify for promotional rates. All rates are clearly disclosed before approval.

Was this helpful?
Agents
How do I become a Shamba Direct agent?

To become an agent, you need to apply through our agent registration portal. We look for individuals with strong community connections and basic agricultural knowledge. Training is provided after selection. Agents earn commissions on sales and can access support materials.

Was this helpful?
What support do agents receive?

Agents receive comprehensive training, marketing materials, and ongoing support from our team. We provide product knowledge resources, sales tools, and regular updates on new products. High-performing agents may qualify for additional incentives and recognition programs.

Was this helpful?
General
What areas do you serve?

We currently serve major agricultural regions in Tanzania, including Arusha, Kilimanjaro, Morogoro, Mbeya, and surrounding areas. We're continuously expanding our coverage. Check our service area map or contact us to confirm availability in your location.

Was this helpful?
How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through WhatsApp at +255 752 607 104, email at huduma@shambadirect.co.tz, or through our chatbot on the website. Our support hours are Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM East Africa Time.

Was this helpful?
Do you have a mobile app?

Yes, we have mobile apps available for both Android and iOS devices. You can download the Shamba Direct app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. The app allows you to browse products, place orders, track deliveries, and access our resources.
